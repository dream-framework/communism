Contours of the Future: The Technological Imperative

Thesis: We hit a civilizational bifurcation. The technical base for democratic techno‑communism already exists inside corporations (real‑time planning, predictive logistics, open ledgers). What remains is political will: either technology serves everyone, or we descend into techno‑feudalism—algorithmic dependency.

1) The paradigm crisis

Late capitalism shifted from exploiting present labor to stealing the future via NPV. When the return requirement r outruns real growth g, the machine breaks.

2) Planning 2.0 is here

  • Corporate planner: Amazon, Walmart—predictive, real‑time logistics as internal plans.
  • Quantum step: logistics/energy optimization; \(N\) qubits encode \(2^N\) states.
  • Transparent accounting: distributed ledgers; algorithmic coordination at scale.

3) Two paths

Techno‑feudalism: rents, castes, behavioral control.
Democratic techno‑communism: needs‑based plan with AI optimization; CLE (ТЭК) as contribution.

4) Why it’s more efficient

  • Removes parasitic ad/finance overhead.
  • Optimizes away crises; resource KPI ↑.
  • Long‑horizon build beats quarterly panic.

No middle ground. “Moderation” delays collapse. The climb is an engineering task.
