Theoretical foundation

Counter‑selection under capitalism is replaced with service‑first eligibility.

CLE (Competency Labor Equivalent)

  • Baseline CLE 5.0–6.0 → all basics guaranteed.
  • High CLE 7.0+ → access to rare missions/resources.
  • Computed by AI from objective labor & civic data.

Scientific basis for selection

~15% stable altruists; up to 60% willing if rules are transparent.

  • Multi‑level psychometrics.

  1. 10+ years of impact; CLE ≥ 7.0.
  2. Neuro‑testing of motives.
  3. One sandbox year under AI audit; immutable ledger of decisions.
  4. Public dossier vote.

Asymmetric transparency

Leaders trade privacy for trust; citizens retain full privacy.

Economics of service

  • Multipliers: 1.2× success, 1.5× outstanding, 0.7× failure.

Cultural shift

Within a generation: trust becomes default; a culture of service displaces greed. A survival‑level reform.
